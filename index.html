Hi i'm Ethan

A young Swimmer

I started swimming training at the age of 7 years old. When I was 9 years old, I was qualified to compete in the Tanzania Junior swimming championships competitions which was held in Dar-es-salaam. It was my first time, and I won silver and bronze medals from relays. Soon after the celebrations of my 10 years birthday on the 25th November, I had an opportunity to attend a lot of other swimming competetions like Isamilo invitational gala, FK blue marlins invitational gala and Tanzania Junior swimming championships July 2022 to continue improving my skills in swimming.

As a young, energetic, ambitious and courageous boy, who wants to pursue my passion and career in swimming, I hereby kindly seek for any donations of any amount to support my training costs, swimming gears and training equipments to improve my performance to qualify to attend Olympics games (2024) for swimming competitions to represent not only myself but also for my country Tanzania. These donations will help alot in boosting my aspirations which already mean winning the competitions and by winning, I aim to return back support to other children like me in Tanzania who have the same aspirations with swimming sports.
